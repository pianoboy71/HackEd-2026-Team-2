ABOUT



We created this website with one goal in mind: to make recycling simple.

Recycling shouldn't feel confusing - but it often is.

Different councils accept different materials, packaging can be unclear, and it's easy to make mistakes that send recyclable items straight to landfill which causes more harm than we realise. We wanted to change that.

Our solution is a start, easy-to-use tool that lets you scan an item and instantly see whether it's recyclable and how to dispose of it correctly. No guessing. No searching. Just clear guidance designed to help you make the right choice every time.

By making recycling easier, we hope to reduce the amount of waste ending up in landfill and support a more sustainable future. Small actions, when taken together, make a big impact and we're here to help everyone play their part.
